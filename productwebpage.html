Whole Young Coconut

  • Whole young coconut is a freshly pick coconut from coconut trees grown in the sounthern part of Vietnam.
  • Color: green.
  • Gross weight: 0.5kg on average.
  • Taste sweet.

Price: 2 EUR/unit

USAGE: open by cutting the bottom part of the whole coconut, through the hard shell of the fruit, until reaching the white coconut meat. Pour coconut water to a cup, serve cool or mix with ice. Waste of coconut can be used as fiber for gardening. Coconut white meat can be eaten right away, or save for making coconut oil.
